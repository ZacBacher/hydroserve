Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about borehole installations and how HydroServe works.

HydroServe offers a completely managed, end-to-end borehole service with zero financial risk to you. We handle everything from the initial site assessment right through to monthly maintenance — and if we don't find water, you don't pay a cent. Our team brings over 25 years of water industry experience to every project.
Municipal water tariffs increase every year, supply is unreliable, and quality is declining in many areas. A borehole gives your property an independent, consistent water supply that isn't affected by municipal outages or price hikes — and it can reduce your water costs by up to 50%.
Yes, completely. We start with a free site assessment. If our analysis shows a strong likelihood of finding groundwater, we proceed with drilling entirely at our own cost. Once we've successfully found water, we will build the whole system and use a state of the art filtration system that we have perfected over the years. You only pay a portion of the savings monthly. No water — no charge.
From the initial assessment to having a fully operational borehole system, the entire process typically takes between 30 and 60 days — depending on geological conditions and any required municipal approvals.
Absolutely. Water safety is something we take incredibly seriously and it's an area where our 25+ years of industry experience really shows. Over the decades we have developed and refined a state of the art filtration system that we have perfected through hundreds of installations across South Africa. Every borehole has a unique water profile, and our system is custom-configured to match it precisely — removing impurities, balancing mineral levels and ensuring your water meets SANS 241 compliance, the official South African standard for safe drinking water. We also carry out thorough water testing throughout the installation process and continue testing every 3 to 6 months thereafter, adjusting the filtration system whenever needed. The result is clean, great-tasting water you can trust straight from the tap.
Your initial assessment is completely free. Because every borehole is different — depth, geology and water quality all vary by site — we can only give you accurate pricing once drilling is complete and the water has been tested.
